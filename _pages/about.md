I am a PhD student at Columbia University, where I am fortunate to be advised by [Christos Papadimitriou](https://www.engineering.columbia.edu/faculty-staff/directory/christos-papadimitriou) and [Mihalis Yannakakis](https://www.cs.columbia.edu/~mihalis/). I work on theoretical computer science, with a focus on algorithmic game theory and machine learning.

Before Columbia, I worked at [CIICADA Lab](https://ciicadalab.github.io/) at the Australian National University, where I also received my B.Sc. Honours in Computer Science. Before that, I received my B.Sc. in Mathematics from the University of Melbourne.

If you are interested in my work, or want to collaborate, please get in touch. My email is `my_first_name (dot) my_last_name @ columbia.edu`. Up-to-date publication lists can also be found at my [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=-8GdFH8AAAAJ&hl=en) and [DBLP](https://dblp.org/pid/263/9577.html).
